Better Beans, Better Coffee

Everyone loves coffee! Our love for coffee brought the birth of the Roasted Ground. We are a local business focusing on exceptional quality coffee blends and Limited Edition single origin offerings.

We source the best locally produced beans. No fertilizers and pesticides were used in the growing and processing of this product. We purchase high quality whole bean coffee exclusively and directly from the small backyard farmers. The beans are roasted utilizing traditional coffee roasting methods striking a balance between important past traditions and today's latest technological advances, always through the lens of an artisanal approach.

Inspired by the relentless passion of the most discerning coffee lovers, our packages honors the quality of the coffee beans inside them. We Invite you to share in our passion.
