Cafe Latte

A Cafe Latte is a type of coffee made with espresso and hot steamed milk, milkier than a cappuccino.

Cafe Mocha

Like a cafe latte, cafe mocha is based on espresso and hot milk, but with Ghirardelli chocolate.

Cafe Americano

A Cafe Americano is a drink of espresso diluted with hot water.

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a double espresso and hot milk, with the surface topped with foamed milk. Don’t want your Cappuccino with milk? Ask us to make your Cap dry…we will leave out the hot milk.

Macchiato

Mixed with only a dash of milk so you still get a strong coffee taste.

Babyccino

Perfect for the little one, this is a warm milk drink, frothed up with steam.
